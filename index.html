I mean, I guess, since I am also in the dorm I get to talk about it as well, even though I’m writing about it, so it is less fair. But I want to take this opportunity to just talk about something interesting, you know?

So this is something of a digital exhibition, there is no real narrative here, since I think that life is not a narrative, and is more like random glimpses of action here and there, that kinda connect to each other after a while.

So on the left you can see Bamango the cat. He’s my favorite dorm cat, btw, cause he’s the most chill and likes to cuddle. The name is funny, tho, cause we used to call every cat Bamanko, and then this one stayed with us, and the girls said that he is mango, so it did stick to the cat, btw, gender of it is still debated, I kinda think this one is intersex.

So from Dodi I know that there are three cats at the dorm. He says that Karcsi the only female one. Mostly keeps to herself and lives a passive lifestyle, or at least tries to since she is harassed multiple times a day by the residents of the dorm. The other two are Noki and Rozsda(Bamango has many names), they are playful and hang around in the smoking yard, they are really friendly and beg for food like Victorian-era child. They are funded mostly by donations, which include their food, medicine and collars, occasional veterinarian visits. They are one of the things that distinguish our dorm from the others, they bring life to the dorm.

But, what I’m getting at is, the cat thingy really shows how our little community formed, since we are quite different, and some are aliens, and a lot of us are quite weird and quirky, yet we do support each other, cook together, share some stories, and I think this is beautiful, and I wish more people to experience at least once.
